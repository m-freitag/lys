An Ode to IBM Plex

IBM Plex is an open source typeface designed by Mike Abbink at IBM: https://www.ibm.com/plex/.

Especially its “sans” variant is gorgeously clear for presentations (and, imho, even better than Firefoxs Fira font). Full math symbols will probably become available soon: https://github.com/IBM/plex/issues/250.

Until then, the -package “mathastext” used in this template works fine, e.g.:

\[y_i \sim N(\mu,\sigma)\]
